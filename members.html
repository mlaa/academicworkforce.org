Membership in CAW
 

Organizations that wish to be part of CAW must meet the following criteria:

  1. The organization’s mission must address concerns that intersect with the work of CAW.
  2. The organization must have been in continuous existence for three years before applying for admission to CAW.
  3. The organization must have a written governance structure that defines terms of office, election or appointment of board members, and other key matters.

Organizations that wish to join CAW should send evidence of having met these criteria, along with samples of any published materials or other relevant documents, to charles.haws@sbl-site.org.
